Strawberries and Cream Oatmeal

"Start your day with a delicious twist - our strawberries and cream oatmeal: a satisfying, sweet surprise that keeps you going strong."

Picture a warm, creamy bowl of oatmeal infused with the luscious sweetness of ripe strawberries. The vibrant red berries, bursting with flavor, add a delightful tangy twist to the smooth, velvety texture of the oatmeal. As you take a spoonful, the sweet aroma of the strawberries mingles with the comforting scent of the creamy oats, creating a breakfast experience that is both indulgent and wholesome. Each bite is a delightful symphony of flavors and textures, a perfect harmony of hearty oats and juicy strawberries, making every morning a delicious celebration of nourishment and joy. Whether enjoyed as a quick weekday breakfast or a leisurely weekend treat, strawberries and cream oatmeal is a delightful way to start the day with a burst of fruity sweetness and comforting creaminess.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups diced strawberries, divided
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar, or to taste
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Place 3/4 cup strawberries, milk, water, brown sugar, and salt in a blender cup and blend until smooth.
  2. Place oatmeal and strawberry mixture in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, add remaining strawberries, and simmer until thick, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in vanilla.
